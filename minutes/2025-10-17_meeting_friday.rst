Operando4NeXus
--------------

Klaus gives a short overview about Operando4NeXus, the successor to SECoP@HMC.
Its intended to expand the capabilities of NeXus for storing sample environment
and make it more flexible. In the HMC project, we produced something good on
the SECoP side, now we have to see to bring this into NeXus.
Need something flexible on the SE side without having static Application
Definitions for each SE.
